## 💡 Full Explanation

Future research must shift the focus of system control from passive monitoring to proactive, autonomous intelligence by building self-reasoning control planes. This involves developing multi-agent systems capable of distributed reasoning to handle complex, real-time operational demands. Crucially, this autonomy must be grounded in formal verification and bounded risk guarantees to ensure safety and reliability. By integrating chaos engineering and cross-organizational knowledge, we can evolve simple observability into true, predictive system intelligence.
